Request Account Deletion

We understand that you may want to delete your Smart Invoice Manager account and associated data. This page provides clear instructions on how to request account deletion and explains what data will be removed.

Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to our support team using the contact button below. Include your registered email address and reason for deletion.

3

Verification Process

For security purposes, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. This helps protect your account from unauthorized deletion requests.

4

Deletion Completion

Account deletion will be processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, authentication data
  • Shop Details: Shop name, address, and business information
  • Invoice Data: All created invoices, customer details, and transaction history
  • Product Information: Saved products and pricing data
  • App Preferences: Settings and customizations
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns and statistics

Data Retention Policy

Immediate Deletion: Most personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion.

Backup Systems: Data in backup systems may take up to 30 days to be completely removed.

Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes for up to 7 years, but this data will be anonymized and not linked to your personal identity.

Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained for app improvement purposes.
